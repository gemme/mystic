WHAT IS M.Y.S.T.I.C.™?

M.Y.S.T.I.C.™

Munitions Yoking Software Technological Information Core

A totally new unique way to acquire, compile, transmit, and track field data. M.Y.S.T.I.C. ™ currently digitizes the tracking and reporting of UXO/MEC EOD information by streaming the collection and dissemination of ordnance data. With evolving and expanding clients needs, our adaptable software is fully capable of integrating client documents encompassing all necessary information, including QC & QA oversight ability and mapping with GPS accuracy.

Reduced field paperwork!

Digitize your safety protocols, munitions, reports, activity logs, photo, flag data, and so much more, while providing a user friendly input process increasing accuracy and enhancing safety in the field.

Smart Reporting

By digitizing the acquisition, compilation, trasnmission, and tracking process, we virtually eliminate reporting delays. M.Y.S.T.I.C.™ enables overall reporting transparency between all parties from the field to the supervisor to final report via a secure cloud or command server. These efficiencies drastically reduce time in the field, manpower and overall costs.

On-Site Training

Available on-site demonstration or training led by Master Training Specialist's includes classroom and field based, hands-on scenario applications.
